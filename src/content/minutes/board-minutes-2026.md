# Board Minutes - 2026

_2026-05-14T18:00:00Z — by nedtwigg_

_Vote conducted by email; meta.mytake.org Discourse is no longer operational. Decision was unanimous among all three officers._

## Context (carried forward from 2025)

- Approximately nobody was using MyTake.org day to day.
- We did not see a viable path to build interest.
- If we shut down, the mytake.org domain could be grabbed by scammers/spammers.
- We tried to find an org to accept the donation, but never got a response after a few rounds of trying different places.

## Changes since 2025

- Around October 2025, we were issued a new debit card, but our various service subscriptions weren't updated to the new card number.
- As a result, the services hosting our website shut it down and deleted some of our data.
- This wasn't noticed until ~February 2026. Ned was able to recover the mailing address and obtain backups of the board minutes, videos, and website.
- We have everything we need to bring the website back online, but it would take a few days of work.
- Silver lining: the mytake.org domain has been dead for months, which makes it less useful to scammers — much safer.

## Decision

The board agrees this has entered the "dangerous neglect" zone. We will shut down MyTake.org, Inc.

## Plan

- Ned will finish the 2025 filings (started today), due Friday.
- The dormant mytake.org domain will be listed for auction at Afternic (the GoDaddy auction site) with a $500 minimum.
- When the domain sells, or by September 1, 2026 — whichever is first — Ned will file the formal dissolution paperwork and donate the remaining checking account balance to one of the charities we previously attempted to donate to.
- Final 2026 tax return will be filed by May 2027.
- All materials will be archived so the site could be resurrected in the future if anyone wanted to do so.
