## IRS Notice

- In 2017, we didn’t include our Schedule B for large donors (over some threshold, ~$1,000)
- They told us “you screwed up, you have 30 days to fix it” - <https://drive.google.com/open?id=1YKNPhDn1bFGmQ5Ovmg-jegLeQNp7oYRZ>
- We fixed it within 30 days - <https://drive.google.com/open?id=1CG_jku_lNmD4OJRYDkAm_XiSoPNjvkNI2yzILAuaGQs>
- Their system says they didn’t receive the response in time
- But, Ned has the receipt that shows that we mailed it within 30 days - <https://drive.google.com/drive/u/2/folders/1itJnQbpCHTXFjqi6jyRT8auGWLUJPzFq>
- Ned filed the paperwork to appeal with the receipt
- And IRS person on the phone apologized and said it should be fixed soon
